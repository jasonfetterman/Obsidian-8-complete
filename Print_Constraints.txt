# OBSIDIAN-8 PRINT CONSTRAINTS (REV A)

## Printer Limits
- Max build volume: 220 x 220 x 250 mm

## Materials
- Prototype: PLA+
- Final: PETG / ABS / Nylon

## Wall Thickness
- Minimum: 2.5 mm
- Structural: 4–6 mm

## Tolerances
- Clearance fit: +0.2 mm
- Tight fit: +0.1 mm

## Orientation Rules
- Layers must run perpendicular to load direction
- Avoid layer separation at joints

## Support Rules
- Avoid supports in:
  - Bolt holes
  - Internal channels

---

# PART SPLITTING STRATEGY

## General Rule
- If part >180 mm → split into multiple pieces

## Split Locations
- Legs: 2–3 segments
- Body: modular panels

## Joint Method
- Bolt-together flanges
- Optional alignment pins

## Strength Rules
- Do NOT split at high-stress points
- Reinforce seams with ribs
