# OBSIDIAN-8 FASTENER STANDARD (REV A)

## Global Rules
- Structural joints: M4 socket head cap screws
- Secondary/light-duty: M3 socket head cap screws
- High-load pivots: M5 bolts

## Materials
- Preferred: Alloy steel (12.9)
- Optional: Stainless (A2-70) for non-structural parts

## Heat-Set Inserts
- Type: Brass heat-set inserts
- M3 → electronics + covers
- M4 → structural parts

## Hole Sizes
- M3 clearance: 3.2 mm
- M4 clearance: 4.2 mm
- M5 clearance: 5.2 mm

## Thread Engagement
- Minimum: 1.5× diameter
  - M3 → 4.5 mm
  - M4 → 6 mm

## Wall Thickness Around Inserts
- M3: 3 mm minimum
- M4: 4.5 mm minimum

## Locking
- Use nylon lock nuts OR Loctite Blue
